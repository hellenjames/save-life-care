DIABETES

Causes:

  1. Type 1 diabetes is caused when an individual's immune system destroys the insulin-producing beta present in the pancreas. This is caused by various factors, viruses that might trigger the disease or genetic and environmental factors.
  2. Reasons like obesity, being overweight, insulin resistance and genetic factors cause type 2 diabetes in individuals.
  3. Gestational diabetes is a type of diabetes caused during pregnancy due to hormonal changes that women go through apart from lifestyle and genetic factors.
  4. Few other reasons that might cause diabetes include genetic mutations, hormonal diseases, usage of medicines or any damage caused to the pancreas or by removing it.

Symptoms:

  1. Unexplainable weight loss.
  2. Having dry skin.
  3. Being very thirsty and hungry.

Treatment:

  1. Physical activity and having a healthy diet.
  2. Self-monitoring.

Ways to Prevent:

  1. One should avoid and skip fad diets and decide on something healthier.
  2. One also needs to be physically active by doing some form of exercise.
  3. One should eat healthier fats and try consuming more plant-based foods.
  4. If one is obese or has extra weight, one should try losing it as soon as possible because extra weight triggers diabetes.
