CANCER

Causes:

  1. Smoking and tobacco
  2. Unhealthy diet and lack of physical activity
  3. Genetics and if someone from the family has cancer
  4. Viruses and other infections

Symptoms:

  1. Fatigue and tiredness
  2. Changes in weight, can be unintended weight gain or weight loss
  3. Persistent cough, indigestion, unexplainable muscle and joint pain, unexplainable night sweats or fever

Treatment:

  1. Physical activity and having a healthy diet.
  2. Self-monitoring.

Ways to Prevent:

  1. One should avoid and skip fad diets and decide on something healthier.
  2. One also needs to be physically active by doing some form of exercise.
  3. One should eat healthier fats and try consuming more plant-based foods.
  4. Regular medical checkups and self-care can also help one prevent cancer.
